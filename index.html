Seedhe Maut

SEEDHE MAUT is an Indian hip-hop duo formed by Siddhant Sharma (Calm) and Abhijay Negi (Encore ABJ). The duo met at Spit Dope Inc., an underground battle rap league in Delhi, where their shared passion for hip-hop brought them together. "This undoubtedly marks their role as trailblazing hip-hop artists in the Indian music scene."

The members are:

  • Siddhant Sharma (Calm)
  • Abhijay Negi (Encore ABJ)
